Effective conservation projects for sea turtles

Sea turtles are magnificent creatures that play a vital role in marine ecosystems. Unfortunately, they face numerous threats such as exploitation, poaching, and pollution. Conservation projects aim to protect these endangered species in locations like Indonesia and Cape Verde. You too can contribute to their preservation by supporting turtle adoption programs.

Protect the sea turtles

The Sea Turtles Association is dedicated to protecting endangered sea turtles in Indonesia and Cape Verde. Our conservation projects focus on combating exploitation, poaching, and pollution to ensure the survival of these magnificent creatures. Join us in our mission to preserve marine ecosystems and support turtle adoption programs.

About Us

The Sea Turtles Association actively engages in beach clean-up initiatives, nesting site protection, and educational programs to raise awareness about the importance of sea turtle conservation. Through community outreach and scientific research, we strive to create a sustainable environment where sea turtles can thrive. Join us in our efforts to safeguard these incredible creatures for future generations.

Our conservation efforts also involve collaborating with local communities to implement sustainable fishing practices that reduce accidental turtle bycatch. We work closely with government agencies to establish protected marine areas and enforce regulations that safeguard sea turtle habitats. By promoting eco-tourism and responsible travel, we aim to create economic opportunities that benefit both local communities and sea turtle populations.
